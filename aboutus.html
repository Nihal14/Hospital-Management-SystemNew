About Us

Sanjeevani Group ventured into healthcare services under the capable leadership of Dr. Ram Sagar in the 80s . We set on the path of providing affordable yet world-class healthcare to their immediate community by founding one of the first corporate, 250 bedded, multi-speciality tertiary care hospital in the city of Mangaluru in the year 2012. This was followed by an ambitious 415 bedded, multi-speciality tertiary care hospital in the year 2023. Sanjeevani Hospitals is synonymous with quality healthcare, efficiency, reliability and has set very high standards in the healthcare sector in the State.
